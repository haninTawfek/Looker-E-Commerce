Welcome to the Looker E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard. This project demonstrates the end-to-end process of transforming raw e-commerce data into strategic business insights to drive revenue growth and improve customer experience.

## ⚙️ ETL & Data Preparation

To ensure data integrity and analytical accuracy, the following steps were performed:

* **Data Integration**: Merged multiple datasets, including **Country Distribution** and **User tables**, to create a holistic view of business performance.
* **Data Cleaning**: Standardized date formats and corrected **Data Types** to ensure accurate time-series analysis.
* **KPI Calculation**: Engineered new metrics and calculated key performance indicators (KPIs) to monitor business health.

## 🔍 Key Insights & Analysis

The analysis of our business data revealed the following:

* **Financial Performance**: Generated a **Net Revenue of $5,972,005.21** with a strong **51.90% Gross Profit Margin**.
* **Sales Scale**: Processed a total of **125,226 orders** resulting in **$10,827,118.91** in total sales.
* **Customer Growth**: Successfully acquired **100,000 customers**, with an **Average Selling Price (ASP) of $59.57** per item.
* **Conversion Metrics**:
* **Purchase Conversion Rate**: 42.06%
* **Marketing Conversion Rate**: 14.67%
* **Item Return Rate**: 10.03%



## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

 **Logistics & Returns Optimization**: An **Item Return Rate of 10.03%** is a critical area for improvement. I recommend conducting a deep dive into shipping data to identify if returns are linked to **shipping delays, damages in transit, or delivery issues**. Improving fulfillment reliability will significantly reduce return costs.
 **Conversion Funnel Strategy**: With a **14.67% Marketing Conversion Rate**, there is significant potential to optimize top-of-funnel acquisition channels. A/B testing landing pages and refining ad targeting could further improve the Purchase Conversion Rate.
 **AOS (Average Order Size) Focus**: Given the **AOS of 1.45**, implementing "Frequently Bought Together" recommendations or volume-based discounts could increase the items per order, directly boosting Net Revenue.

🖥️ Interactive Dashboard

The dashboard was developed using a custom design inspired by Figma, ensuring a modern, user-friendly interface. It features interactive slicers that allow users to drill down by **Order Status** and **Time Periods**, facilitating data-driven decision-making.
